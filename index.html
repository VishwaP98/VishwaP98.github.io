Projects

PetConnect - Android Application

PetConnect is an Android application designed by a team of 3 to improve delivery of Animal Services of City of Toronto. It includes a map of different parks and recreational locations in Toronto. Having the official website of Animal Services in the app, you are always notified of the City's initiatives. Alongside, the app also has a reminder feature that extracts data from the City's database, generates a list of all the events for the month and gives a reminder whenever an event is nearby.

Cave Escape - First Android App

Cave Escape is an Android gaming application developed by a team of 2 in which level of difficult increases as game progresses. The app builds a high score table onto the device saving the player's high score. Moreover, the application has gems that you can collect to give yourself an extra life. The game also tracks the number of gems just like the high score. This game has been downloaded by more than 100 users on the Play Store and receiving great responses on my first Android app
